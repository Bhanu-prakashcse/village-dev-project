DRILLING OF BORE WELLS

Project Overview

The project was implemented in Mangupalli Village of Nellore district. The poor SC families who had lands were kept ideally without water facilities. This borewell water will help irrigate their lands, improve yield, and combat poverty. Water is essential for agriculture, and with its increasing scarcity, the project aims to enhance environmental balance, improve land cultivation, and support women's income generation programs.

  • The introduction of borewell water ensures a consistent water supply, enabling the cultivation of multiple crops throughout the year.
  • Improved crop yields lead to enhanced food security and increased income for farmers, boosting their overall well-being.
  • The project promotes sustainable water use, preventing soil erosion, maintaining soil fertility, and supporting biodiversity.
  • Empowering women by providing water accessibility allows them to engage in various agricultural and allied activities, fostering gender equality and enhancing their socio-economic status.
  • Community participation is encouraged through training programs on efficient water use, modern agricultural techniques, and sustainable farming practices.
  • These initiatives equip the community with the knowledge and skills required to maximize the benefits of the borewell water and ensure the long-term success of the project.
  • The project is a comprehensive approach to rural development, addressing the immediate needs of poor SC families while promoting environmental sustainability, gender empowerment, and community development.

    • Significant improvements in agricultural productivity, economic stability, and overall quality of life for the residents of Mangupalli Village are expected.
