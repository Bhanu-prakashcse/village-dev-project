Message from the Founder

Dear Friends and Supporters,

I am proud to say that V.D.S is committed to the cause of the children and women who are more vulnerable. In this respect we need to go still a long way. Concerted efforts are to begeared to counteract the marginalization of women, by which women are emancipated. Since emancipation of women is a Herculean task, financial resources and human resources and work able network are a must. V.D.S gratefully acknowledges the support of donors and well-wishers.We extend our sincere thanks and profound appreciation to all our Board Members for their valuable guidance and support. Our sincere & heart-felt thanks would go to all our staff and volunteers for their dedicated and valuable services.

Best regards,

Kaluva Narshimhulu
