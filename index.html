Our Vision

V.D.S envisions a just self-reliant community of men and women who are socio-economic and environmentally conscious with an awareness to respect the basic human rights of each individual.

Our Mission

To empower the rural and marginalized community through sustainable and inclusive development programs by working with active participation of stakeholders.

Goal

To transform the lives of vulnerable groups and marginalized communities and help them live with dignity and respect.

"Eradication Of Social In Equalities"
