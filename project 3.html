Drinking Water Project

This project was implemented in Rammannagaripalle Village of Nellore District. A bore well will be installed by drilling to a depth of 225 feet at the center of the target people's houses. A water plant will be constructed with a height of 6 feet near the bore well. At the bottom of the water tank, 10 taps will be arranged to draw water. Beneficiaries will be equipped with skills and knowledge to carry out health and sanitation education activities and participate in the regular maintenance of hand pumps, motors, and taps to ensure a sustainable supply of clean and safe drinking water.
