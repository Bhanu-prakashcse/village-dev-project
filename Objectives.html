Our Objectives

  • To provide educational guidance to the youth to choose necessary courses to build up their careers in the future.
  • To identify drop-out children and never-enrolled children, motivate them and their parents, and mainstream them into regular schools.
  • To conduct, organize, and run Vocational Training Centers to improve skills among unemployed youth to enhance their employment opportunities.
  • To develop backward villages and weaker sections of people through integrated socio-economic activities in agriculture, horticulture, irrigation, watershed development, and improve the living conditions of villagers and slum dwellers.
  • To work for the protection and promotion of the environment in areas such as Disaster Mitigation Management, Drought Relief activities, Promotion of Non-conventional Energy sources, watershed development, Protection of Forest Eco-system, and other related activities.
  • To organize health and medical camps for health awareness and provide free medicines for curable diseases.
  • To provide comprehensive health and nutritious services by setting up community health facilities, mobile medical care, disabled care, and Day Care Centers for children and handicapped persons.
  • To start and manage Skill Development Training Centers to improve employable skills among unemployed youths.
  • To educate adolescent boys and girls on physical changes, RTI, STI, HIV/AIDS, and other reproductive health matters.
  • To improve eco-friendly technologies like the conservation of potable water, utilization of solar energy, and community cow dung gas units.
  • To work for the eradication of child labor in society.
  • To encourage sustainable agriculture methods and Natural Pest Management methods to avoid the use of chemical fertilizers and pesticides.
  • To initiate internal lending among self-help groups to meet their emergency needs and capital requirements for starting income-generating activities.
