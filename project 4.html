COVID- 19 Emergency Relief

V.D.S Team distributed the food essential commodities viz., Rice, Wheat Flour, Cooking Oil, Sugar, Salt, Dhal, Dry coconut, Tamarind, Chili Powder, Vegetables, Fruits and Hygiene items viz., Masks, Soaps and Hand washing Liquids, to 750 poor households at 50 villages of Nellore District, Andhra Pradesh State. The distributed commodities feed these households for up to one month during the lockdown period. In addition to the food items distribution, V.D.S Team conducted awareness to the village people on how to effectively prevent the Corona virus. The Health Worker organized Awareness Creation among the People Regarding the Safe Practices for Avoiding Corona virus Infection.

The meeting aim is to educate individuals on health concerns, social issues, environmental challenges, or organizational policies. The objective is to provide valuable information, encourage thoughtful discussion, and inspire action. By presenting facts, statistics, personal stories, and expert insights, organizers aim to enlighten the audience, leading to better decision-making, more informed opinions, and proactive behavior. They often include Q&A sessions, group discussions, or interactive activities to engage participants and foster dialogue. Awareness Meetings offer several benefits, such as raising consciousness about overlooked or misunderstood subjects, encouraging proactive behavior, and fostering a well-informed community.
